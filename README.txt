Talika is a free online appointment and scheduling plugin to manage your services and staff.

== Description ==

Talika is an easy to use free appointment and scheduling plugin suitable for any business niche offering a range of services to their customers. It is ideal for a spa, salons, wellness center, yoga center, clinic, gyms and many more.

Using this plugin, you can easily list all your services, manage all your staff and offer an easy way to book appointments to your customers.
